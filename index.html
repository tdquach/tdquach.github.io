Who I Am

A foodie and a tech junkie.

I was born and raised in Fresno, California. I achieved my Bachelor of Science in Business Administration Computer Information Systems from CSU Fresno in 2016. I currently work as a Business Systems Analyst II for the County of Fresno. I have always been intrigued by technology and chose to take a career path that would involve working with computers and technology.

Foodie

I love food, whether it is cooking or eating it. I like to try new foods and random flavors. I always try to have a taste of a local dish whenever I travel somewhere.

Adventurer

I enjoying traveling and visiting new places. I also enjoy taking trips to visit new hiking trails. It is always an eye opening experience traveling to another country and seeing how different things are. I have not taken that many international trips but I plan to take more soon.

Music Lover

I love listening to all types of music. Music helps me throughout the day, long car rides, and at the gym. I enjoy going to concerts and music festivals. Whether it is Coachella or EDC, I enjoy the vibes and discovering new artists at those festivals.
